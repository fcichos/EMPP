    ğŸ’¡ Common Student Questions

    Q: Why are the equations so similar?
    A: Both use polar coordinates for central forces. The mathematical structure (centrifugal + radial force + coupling) is the same; only the specific force differs.

    Q: How do I know if my solution is correct?
    A: Check: (1) Energy conservation, (2) Angular momentum conservation (planets), (3) Compare to analytical solution if available, (4) Physical reasonableness.

    Q: What causes chaos in the spring pendulum?
    A: Nonlinear coupling between modes + sufficient energy. Not all parameter choices give chaos!

    Q: Why donâ€™t we see chaos in planetary motion?
    A: Single planet around Sun is â€œintegrableâ€ (has enough conserved quantities). Multi-planet systems can be chaotic!

    Q: Can I simulate the whole solar system?
    A: Yes! But you need the N-body equations (Week 7+ material). Each planet affects all others.
